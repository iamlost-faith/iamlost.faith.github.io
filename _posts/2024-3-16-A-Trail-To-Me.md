> A trail to me.

There were once two cities at war, but one city could not stand it any longer. So this city decided to strategise for the next battle.
The councelor of the king in this being-defeated-city had come and told the king, "king, this war is too fierce. If we continue to fight like this, it may become the end of us. We should try something else." The king then starting to think of things to do, came up with the idea of killing all the dogs. "In this town, there are many dogs." He said. "We should kill all of them and then we will be safe from our enemies."
