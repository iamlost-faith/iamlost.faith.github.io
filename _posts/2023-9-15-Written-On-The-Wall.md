> Written On The Wall

One day, a street artist was painting a murral. When he finished, he left.<br>

The start.<br>

## Meaning

When a street artist leaves a painting on a wall, does he know the type of person who will stop to look at it?<br>
Does he know how they will feel?<br>
What they will think?<br>

He doesn't know them. He doesn't see them. Yet he paints for them.<br>

Faith is knowing these people exist even if you don't see them. And because they do, street artists paint on walls.<br>

We haven't seen God's ears stretching out from heaven to sit in front of our mouths when we pray.<br>
We don't know how God feels when we paint tears, when we thank in joy.<br>
We don't know what He thinks when we close our eyes, when we lift our arms, when we fall on our knees.

What we know is when we finish, and say "Amen!", it starts becoming it. The rest is testimony.
